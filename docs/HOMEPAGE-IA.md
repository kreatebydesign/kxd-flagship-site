## Hero Composition

```
[Eyebrow / meta]          EST. 2020 · Portland, Oregon (or live location)

LUXURY                    ← Cormorant, uppercase, stacked
WEBDESIGN                 ← second line, same display system

Precision. Clarity. Presence.
This is digital luxury by KXD.

[CONTACT US]  [VIEW CASES]

SCROLL ↓
```

- Background: `#0a0a0a` → `#070707` gradient + gold radial glow + noise overlay
- No sentence-case soft headline
- No blue primary button — gold gradient or white ghost

## Services — "Built Beyond Standards."

- Section label: OUR SERVICES
- Headline: **Built Beyond Standards.**
- Supporting: Strategy. Design. Execution. — when everything aligns.
- Numbered service cards (01, 02, 03…) with uppercase titles
- Luxury web development remains first / primary in ordering

## Case Studies — "Proof Over Promises."

- Section label: OUR CASES
- Headline: **Proof Over Promises.**
- Card structure (from live site):
  - Industry label (gold, uppercase) — MOTOR RACING, LUXURY HOSPITALITY
  - Client name (Cormorant, uppercase)
  - VIEW PROJECT action
  - Tagline repeat: Precision. Clarity. Presence.
  - Year
- Dark `#111` cards, `#2a2a2a` borders — not soft rounded SaaS cards

## Partner Wall

- Label: TECHNOLOGY PARTNERS or OUR CLIENTS
- Grid of logos — grayscale, muted, sharpen on hover
- Minimal chrome, no carousel noise on desktop

## Final CTA — "Let's Build What Others Can't."

- Headline: **Let's Build What Others Can't.**
- Body: We partner with brands that are serious about building at the highest level.
- Primary: Start a Project →
- Secondary: direct email (matt@kreatebydesign.com)

## Strategic Notes

- Luxury websites stay the primary entry in hero and services order
- Platform/enterprise capability is not a homepage hero message — it lives on `/platforms`
- KXD OS is not referenced on the homepage
